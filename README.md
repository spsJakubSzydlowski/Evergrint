# Evergrint

## About

Evergrint is a 2D RPG game made in Godot Engine inspired by games like Terraria, Forager and Minecraft. Explore the world, fight enemies, gather better items and summon a boss underground!

## Play

Itch.io: https://kubki.itch.io/evergrint

## HOW TO PLAY

DIFFICULTIES

    Easy - the intended difficulty of the game.
    Hard - enemies have 1.5x damage and health, and some enemies have additional attacks.

ITEMS AND INVENTORY

    Use items - put your item in the hotbar (the first row of the inventory),  then either scroll with your mouse to the desired slot or press 1-9 on your keyboard and click with the left mouse button.

    Move items in the inventory - click on an item, drag it over the desired slot, then click again to place it.

    Heal - apples can be used for healing. You can also press H on your keyboard to quick heal if apples are in your inventory.

ENEMIES

    Slime - a chasing enemy that drops weapons, green apples, and boss summon item when killed. Spawns on the Surface around the player.
    Mole - the first boss, can be summoned with the Head of the Burrower (drops from slimes) in the Underground.

WORLDS

    Surface - the default world with trees and water.
    Underground - you can teleport there by right-clicking the sinkhole on the surface. There is stone that you can mine with a pickaxe.

HOW TO WIN?

    Demo ends after killing the Mole boss.
